Welcome to the Ninjago Matching Game!

Directions:

  1. Click on cards to reveal what image is behind them.
  2. Each time you click a card, your score will increase by one.
  3. Try to click on two cards that are a match.
  4. The best score you can get is a 16, since there are 16 cards.
  5. The closer your score is to 16, the better. Try to match them in as few moves as possible.

Good luck!
